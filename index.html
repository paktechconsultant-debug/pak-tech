Who We Are

Engineering Excellence Since 2025

Pak-Tech Engineering Consultant (Pvt) Ltd is a forward-thinking consultancy firm established in 2025, headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan. We specialize in providing high-quality engineering, technology, and business consultancy services to corporate, public, and private sector clients.
